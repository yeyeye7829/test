Project Description
This webpage is built around music and my life.
The flowing essence of music is the displacement of time. I feel that the music genres I
listen to in each stage of life represent the mood of that stage.
I want to use flowers to visualize my musical timeline — matching different mental
stages and their corresponding genres with flowers. Through this floral visualization.
I express my social state, creative input, emotions, and energy during each stage.
The cards below represent each period: stage, mood, color, music genre, and genre
characteristics."
